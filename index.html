What Our Student Says

Name:pooja patil | M.Tech. in Process Instrumentation - Batch of 2016

1.Placements: About 60-70% of the students from our course got placed. Top recruiting companies like Tata Motors, Philips, Fiat, Cognizant, Capgemini, etc., visit our college for placements. The highest salary package offered was 10 LPA
2.Infrastructure: The infrastructure of the college is unique and awesome. Labs and classrooms are well-equipped. The library has good books.
3.Faculty: Teachers in our college are well-qualified. Some teachers are PhD holders. They help students with their final year projects and also with an internship

Name:Anuj Sabale | M.Tech. in Digital Systems - Batch of 2016

1.Placements: Honestly, as compared to Btech, placements for Mtech are less. For my batch, for digital system, almost 15 out of 18 got placed. Companies like Seagate and Wavelate, which are core companies, came for internships.
2.Infrastructure: As this college is the top college in Pune, there is no doubt about the infrastructure of this college. All departments are well equipped.
3.Faculty: I was in the Electronics and Telecommunications department. In this department, all faculty members are very helpful, qualified and knowledgeable.

Name:Saurabh Masal | B.Tech. in Computer Engineering - Batch of 2022

1.Placements: More than 90% of the students from the computer engineering branch got placed. In other streams also a good percentage of students got placed.
2.Infrastructure: The infrastructure of our college is good. All necessary equipment is available in labs. Wi-Fi facility is good
3.Faculty: The course curriculum is upgraded every time. Faculty have a good idea of industry and up-gradation of industry. On commencement day, they make students ready to step into the industry.
